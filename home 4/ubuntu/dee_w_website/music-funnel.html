Meet Damone Ward Sr. - Gifted Eternity

Professional musician, singer, songwriter, producer, and rapper with a passion for creating music that touches the soul and inspires the mind. Certified in Christian Counseling, Mediation, and behavioral work, Damone channels life experience, spiritual insight, and emotional intelligence into every song.

With three dynamic albums showcasing versatility and evolution, Gifted Eternity creates music that speaks to the heart, motivates the mind, and connects with people across all walks of life.
